About Me!

Software Engineer

Im a junior web developer who enjoys building clean,structured, and functional web experiences. Im especialaly interested in how logic, design, and usability come together to create applications that are not only technically solid but also easy and pleasant to use. For me, writing code is not just about making things work, but about making them understandable, maintaainable, and meaningful.

Graphic Designer

Alongside web development, I have a strong interest in graphic design. This helps me think visually when working on interfaces, layouts, and overall presentation. I enjoy paying attention to spacing, hierarchy, and clarity so that users can interact with a product intuitively. Combining design thinking with development allows me to approach problems from both a technical and visual perspective..

Teaching

I also like teaching and sharing knowledge. I like explaining concepts in a simple and structured way, especially when dealing with topics that may feel complex at first. Whether through discussions, small guides, or examples, I find satisfaction in helping others understand how things work. Teaching also helps me deepen my own understanding and improve how I communicate ideas.

Writing

Writing is where I slow down and think deeply. I use writing to break down complex ideas, reflect on my learning journey, and express thoughts that may not always fit into code. It helps me sharpen my understanding and communicate ideas more clearly, both for myself and for others.
